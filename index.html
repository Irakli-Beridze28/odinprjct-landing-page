Leopard 2A7V - Main Battle Tank

The Leopard 2A7V is advanced variant of the German Leopard 2 main battle tank, developed by Krauss-Maffei Wegmann (KMW). Introduced in 2019, the A7V is designed for high-intensity warfare and urban combat, building on the proven Leopard 2 platform with significant upgrades in firepower, protection, and digital systems.

Leopard 2 Variants
Leopard 2 Tank Prototype - Design tested for improved armour, firepower, and mobility.
Leopard 2A4 - Standardized across NATO with a new digital fire control system and improved turret armor.
Leopard 2A5 - Recognized for it's angular spaced turret armour, enhanced overall defensive capabilities.
Leopard 2A6 - Equipped with a longer 120mm L55 smoothbore gun, significantly increasing firepower and range.
